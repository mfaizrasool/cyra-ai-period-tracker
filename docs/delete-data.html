Delete your data — Cyra Period Tracker

Cyra stores your cycle and health logs on your device only. You can delete everything at any time without contacting support.

Delete all data in the app

  1. Open Cyra Period Tracker on your phone.
  2. Go to the Profile tab.
  3. Tap Reset app & delete all data.
  4. Confirm Delete everything.

This permanently removes period logs, daily logs, cycle settings, reminders, and other saved preferences from that device. It cannot be undone.

Firebase analytics & crash data

Cyra may send anonymous usage and crash reports to Google Firebase. To limit this, use your device privacy or advertising settings, or uninstall the app.
